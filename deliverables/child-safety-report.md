Per-page child safety notes:

- FL-HTML-02: Adult-only authentication; no child-facing encouragement to enter data.
- FL-HTML-03: No full name, photo, location, school, or contact request.
- FL-HTML-04: Age bands prevent reading-heavy UI for non-readers.
- FL-HTML-05: No scary imagery, conflict, ads, or manipulative countdowns.
- FL-HTML-06: No external links, purchases, ads, chat, or adult settings in child UI.
- FL-HTML-07: No streak pressure, no variable reward loop, no social comparison.
- FL-HTML-08: No infinite autoplay; next story requires parent decision.
- FL-HTML-09: Activity uses safe household object and adult supervision.
- FL-HTML-10: Does not grade the child; accepts pointing, sound, or silence.
- FL-HTML-11: No comparison, ranking, public sharing, or pressure metrics.
- FL-HTML-12: No autoplay, no streak, no bright reward, no endless scroll.
